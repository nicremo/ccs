# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want CCS to be a useful and focused open-source project. Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate respectfully and keep discussions technical, clear, and actionable.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and respectful.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused.
- Provide evidence for bug reports.
- Assume maintainers may ask for reproduction steps before accepting a claim.
- Avoid posting secrets, tokens, or private configuration.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Spam or unrelated promotion.
- Repeated off-topic comments.
- Posting real API keys, tokens, or credentials.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete comments and may block contributors who repeatedly violate this policy.
